some info on the my 'career'

My first interaction with computers started with a spiderman video game I picked up my first year of kindergarden. It fulfilled me and entertained me so much that the whole time I was at school I just anticipated going home and playing it. My mother however limited me to one hour of video games for a few years until she loosened her grip and I could moderate myself around the fifth grade. My academic success has never existed. Throughout elementry school it was because I for some reason cant stay focused very well. When the middle school era came around the teaching was done in a way that kind of suited me, but my grades were still dead because they had serious homework assignments, and their was no way in hell I was going home and doing work rather than play Xbox.

Throughout middle school I had no choice but to play Xbox because I did not have the funds to build a PC, but I always envied them PC aryans because they got all of the cool Minecraft updates years before us console peasants did. I was lucky enough to meet Jay Ashworth who somehow had his leg up on computer gaming. He took me under his wing and showed me the way of the PC master race. For years I trained. Adventuring to the lengths and depths of Midgard, fasting 40 days multiple times until I had enough shekels to forge my Computer.

The summer break between eight and ninth grade I finally did it, but I didnt know how to put an OS on it. Once again I needed Jays mind to teach me. He told me how to put Windows 7 on it and from there I tought myself how to do basic things like that. A year later I was making my own Call of Duty: World at War zombies map. Then in the tenth grade I started making bare bones html websites, and a year later Ms. Sinclair started teaching me how to make websites with purpose. THem at the end of the school year she assigned me a project in which I have to talk about myself. Yeah!
